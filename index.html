While life can be confusing and new, YouDoYou is a place where you can come and get helpful advice for all of your social, academic, physical and mental self care issues. About Bullying

What is bullying? What can you do about it?

According to www.stopbullying.gov bullying is any unwanted, aggressive behavior that involves a power imbalance. Bullying comes in many forms and is not just physical abuse, purposefully leaving people out or embarrassing them repeatedly is also considered bullying. This behavior comes in three different forms: verbal bullying, social bullying and physical bullying. If you think you or someone you know is being bullied these links below will help you figure out if what you are experiencing is bullying and what to do about it.

  • What is bullying?: https://www.stopbullying.gov/what-is-bullying/index.html#types
  • Getting help: http://www.mentalhealthamerica.net/bullying-what-do-if-im-bullied
